The values that guide my life:

Respect - I believe that respect is one of the most important values in interpersonal relationships. Especially in my family, respect has always been a top priority. A person who does not respect their surroundings will not be able to respect themselves.
Honesty and Transparency - I always strive to be open and honest with myself and with those around me, whether it's friends, family, colleagues, or classmates. I believe that honesty builds genuine connections based on trust.
Helping Others - I believe that we have the power to help one another, even through small acts. A small gesture of help can be very meaningful to someone else and create a real connection.
Responsibility - I believe that taking responsibility for our actions is a fundamental value. Even if I make a mistake, I take responsibility because it builds trust with others and leads to success in any field we engage in.
